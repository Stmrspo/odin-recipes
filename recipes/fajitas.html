Fajitas

Description

Delicious Tex-Mex fajitas. As easy as it is delicious, these are sure to please any palate.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Steak or Chickenn
  • 2 Sweet Onions
  • 2 Bell peppers, colors of your choice
  • 2 Tsp Cumin
  • 1 Tbs Chili Powder
  • Red Pepper to taste
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Sautee meat on high heat in oil, remove from heat and drain.
  2. Sautee sliced onions and Peppers on high heat, until starting to carmelize
  3. Add spices, cook 1 more minute
  4. Return meat to pan, and cook until sauce is thickened
  5. Serve with Tortillas or over rice
