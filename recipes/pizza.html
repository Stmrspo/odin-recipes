Pizza

Description

APIZZA! A delightful rustic pizza dough, best when used in a high temp pizza oven.

Ingredients

  • 500 g Flour
  • 275 g Water
  • 2 g Active dry yeast
  • 15 g Olive oil
  • 14 g sea salt

Steps:

  1. Add Flour, water and yeast into mixer. Mix 2 min.
  2. Add Olive oil and salt, mix 2 min.
  3. Let dough rest for 10 mins.
  4. Knead dough for 5 - 10 minutes, until 'window-pane' effect is achieved.
  5. Let dough Bulk Rest for 2 hours, then ball and refrigerate.
  6. Remove from Fridge 2 hours before baking.
  7. Open dough, and top with desired toppings.
  8. Bake hot and fast!
