Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious recipe, surely to be enjoyed by the entire family!

Ingredients

  • 1 Lb Sausage
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • onion
  • Basil
  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese
  • a shitload of garlic

Steps:

  1. Brown Sausage, drain and set aside.
  2. Dice onions and sautee in olive oil until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and sautee one more minute.
  4. Add tomatoes, cooked sausage and other spices.
  5. Layer meat, ricotta, mozz and cooked noodles in a pan.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees F for one hour.
