Banana Oatmeal

Description

Creamy oatmeal with sweet banana. What could be better??

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Oatmeal
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • cinnamon to taste

Steps:

  1. Add milk, banana, butter and cinnamon to a pot
  2. Bring to a simmer, add oatmeal and turn heat to low.
  3. simmer until desired thickness is reached, serve.
